About

Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Lab


Welcome to the Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) Lab, led by Prof. Jaepil Ko in the Department of Computer Engineering at the esteemed College of Engineering, Kumoh National Institute of Technology.
Our dedicated research team delves into the realms of deep learning, computer vision, and pattern recognition to push the boundaries of visual intelligence and advance various applications in fields such as image processing, semantic segmentation, and acoustic signal processing. Join us on our journey to unravel the mysteries of visual perception and unlock the potential of cutting-edge technologies that bridge the gap between machines and the visual world.

Interests

Our Research Interests Include

Computer Vision

Explore cutting-edge research in computer vision algorithms, deep learning models, and image understanding.

Image Processing

Dive into the realm of image processing techniques and algorithms to enhance and manipulate visual information.

Semantic Segmentation

Achieve pixel-level understanding of images and extract meaningful insights for various applications

Acoustic Signal Processing

Delve into the unraveling the mysteries of acoustic signal processing, sound analysis and speech recognition
